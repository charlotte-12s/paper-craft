# ECCV — European Conference on Computer Vision

## Basic Info
- Full name: European Conference on Computer Vision
- CCF Level: A
- Acceptance rate: ~27% (2024: ~27%)
- Page limit: 14 pages + supplementary
- Deadline: Typically March (notification July)
- Double-blind review: Yes

## Review Weights
- Innovation: 25% — Rigorous formulation valued
- Theory: 25% — Mathematical formulation appreciated
- Empirical: 30% — More pages = more thorough experiments expected
- Clarity: 20% — European style rigor in presentation

## Writing Style
- Title: Can be more descriptive (more pages available)
- Abstract: Detailed motivation + method overview
- Introduction: More thorough background and motivation (European style)
- Experiments: Comprehensive with mathematical formulation

## Recent Trends (2024-2026)
- Efficient vision models
- Geometric deep learning
- Video understanding
- 3D vision and neural rendering
- Vision for autonomous driving

## Reviewer Common Concerns
- "Insufficient depth — you have 14 pages, use them"
- "Mathematical formulation is missing or informal"
- "Not enough experimental analysis"
- "Method section too brief for the page budget"

## Anti-patterns
- Shallow treatment of method
- Not utilizing the generous page budget
- Missing mathematical formulation
- Incomplete experimental analysis

## Success Patterns
- Rigorous formulation + comprehensive experiments
- Thorough analysis using the full page budget
- Mathematical grounding with empirical validation
- Detailed method section with formal treatment
